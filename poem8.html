• Sonnet (1979)
  • caught -- the bubble
  • in the spirit level,
  • a creature divided;
  • and the compass needle
  • wobbling and wavering,
  • undecided.
  • Freed -- the broken--
  • thermometer's mercury
  • running away;
  • and the rainbow-bird
  • from the narrow bevel
  • of the empty mirror,
  • flying wherever
  • it feels like, gay!
  • Elizabeth Bishop
