Sonnet (1979)

caught -- the bubble

in the spirit level,

a creature divided;

and the compass needle

wobbling and wavering,

undecided.

Freed -- the broken--

thermometer's mercury

  1. running away;
  2. and the rainbow-bird
  3. from the narrow bevel
  4. of the empty mirror,
  5. flying wherever
  6. it feels like, gay!
 

Elizabeth Bishop
