Sonnet (1979)

  1. caught -- the bubble
  2. in the spirit level,
    • a creature divided;
    • a the compass needle
    1. wobbling and wavering,
    2. undecided.

Freed -- the broken--

thermometer's mercury

running away;

and the rainbow-bird

from the narrow bevel

of the empty mirror,

flying wherever

it feels like,  gay!

 
  • Elizabeth Bishop
